## What Is Vendor Managed Inventory (VMI)?

Vendor Managed Inventory (VMI) is a supply chain solution where Masline manages your electronic parts inventory directly, ensuring the right components are always stocked.

Masline’s VMI includes:

- Continuous monitoring of stock levels  
- Automatic purchase order initiation  
- Real-time demand and consumption forecasting  
- JIT (Just-in-Time) component availability  
- Min/max planning for long-term reliability  

With VMI, companies eliminate procurement bottlenecks while improving cost savings and planning accuracy.
